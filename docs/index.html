Version 0.1.1

The first playtest build focuses on reliable tracking and GUI feedback. It does not apply combat bonuses yet; that comes after the XP pacing and interface are proven in game.

What It Does

Per-turret progression

Tracks XP, level, total XP, kills, and lifetime damage for each vanilla gun turret.

Combat feedback

Shows HP, attack speed, range, loaded ammo, and estimated ammo damage in a compact panel.

Early balance pass

Awards XP from turret damage and a small bonus on kills while leaving vanilla stats unchanged.

Playtest Path

Install from the in-game Mods interface, place a vanilla gun turret, add ammo, open it, and confirm the Turret XP panel appears on the right.

  1. Let the turret shoot enemies.
  2. Check that XP and lifetime damage increase.
  3. Confirm kills increase when the turret lands killing blows.
  4. Report confusing stats, pacing issues, or runtime errors.
